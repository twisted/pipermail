                                                                from the ambient air around it without depleting 
                                                                the traditional water supplies. This is our 
                                                                first international placement but watch for more 
                                                                once the environmental impact of our AWG is

                                                                <p><i><font face="Verdana" color="#000080">About 
                                                                Water Generators </font></i></p>
                                                                <p><i><font face="Verdana" color="#000080">The 
                                                                WaterPure Atmospheric Water Generator extracts 
                                                                moisture from the atmosphere through a 
                                                                condensation process and transforms it into 
                                                                absolutely pure, healthy drinking water. 
                                                                Multiple air and water filtration systems remove 
                                                                particulate matter smaller than .01 microns. 
                                                                Utilizing high intensity UV (ultra violet), it 
                                                                eliminates any microorganisms including bacteria 
                                                                and viruses. Test results of WaterPure water 
                                                                measured<b> 99.9% purity</b>, <b>far exceeding 
                                                                EPA requirements</b>. Operating on standard 110v 
                                                                power in the USA, it is extremely efficient and 
                                                                uses a minimal amount of energy to produce 
                                                                water. Depending on local electricity costs, <b>
                                                                a gallon of WaterPure water costs about 8 cents 
                                                                to produce.</b> The unit requires no plumbing, 
                                                                water lines, or pipes and is easily installed. 
                                                                Driven by a microcomputer control system, it 
                                                                will stop generating water when full. The 
                                                                WaterPure purification system employs special 
                                                                filters to remove any unpleasant tastes or odor 
                                                                that may be present in the air. The result is 
                                                                fresh and delicious drinking water in its purest 
                                                                form. </font></i></p>
