But you are misusing rendering machinery.

<div n:data="foo">
    <h2><span n:data="1" n:render="string" /></h2>
   <h3><span n:data="2" n:render="mapping"><n:slot name="foo.2" 
/><n:slot name="foo.2bis" /></span></h3>
</div>

works and does what you need.
