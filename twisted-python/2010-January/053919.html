> terms of addCallbacks. Roughly:
> 
> def passthru(arg):
>     return arg
> 
> d.addCallback(cb) is equivalent to d.addCallbacks(cb, passthru)
> 
> d.addErrback(eb) is equivalent to d.addCallbacks(passthru, eb)
