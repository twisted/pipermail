As some of you may have noticed, the Author section of the divunal.com web page
is now password protected, to keep out normal players and other people who
shouldn't be there.

That doesn't include the recipients of the Author list, however,  :-)  so
simply enter your god character's name (in lower case) for the User ID, and
your god password as your password.

I.e. If your character is Bob, and his password is "mAdHaX0r", you would enter:

User ID: bob
Password: mAdHaX0r

To get into the Author's section.

Sorry for any inconvenience this may have caused.
